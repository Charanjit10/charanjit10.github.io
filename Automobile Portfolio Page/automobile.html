KTM AG (Kronreif & Trunkenpolz Mattighofen), formerly KTM Sportmotorcycle AG is an Austrian motorcycle, bicycle and sports car manufacturer owned by Pierer Mobility AG and the Indian manufacturer Bajaj Auto.
